"Be still and know that I am God." — Psalm 46:10

Experience a guided journey into stillness with the Silence & Sacred Listening app. Created for the Women's Fellowship of Fellowship Community Church, this resource offers a space to quiet the internal noise and focus on divine direction. Choose between a single "Practice of the Day" or commit to a multi-day journey to deepen your prayer life.
